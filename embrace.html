the faint smell of smoke pierces through the air
you see a flower on the ground, bathed in soft, orange warmth.

even amongst the flames, it remains entirely still, its form unchanging.
however, you can't help but wonder, does this warmth truly do more good than harm?

embrace it

believing this to be the right answer for the flower, you decide to embrace it.
the flames slowly engulf you as well, surrounding you in a warm light.

your hands remain intact, as does the flower, however, as the warmth pierces
through your skin you feel a searing pain slowly creeping into your senses.

as you watch the petals dance amidst the blaze and the drifting ashes
you can't help but worry these flames may one day engulf it whole.

put it out

worried that the flames may eventually scorch the flower, you decide to put it out.
the warm light slowly fades away, leaving behind only a charred, yet beautiful form.

you watch as the petals fall off one by one, and break into dust.
one of the petals flies towards you, and singes your hand slightly.

eventually, all that remains is a pile of flickering cinders, however,
you feel as if you will never forget this flower's warmth.
